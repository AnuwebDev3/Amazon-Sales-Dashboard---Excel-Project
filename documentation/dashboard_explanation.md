Amazon Sales Dashboard Project

1.Skills Demonstrated:

   a. Data cleaning: filtering, organizing, and preparing Amazon product data for analysis

   b. Pivot tables: calculating top ratings, review counts, discount percentages, and combined rating + discount comparisons

   c. Pivot charts: bar charts, column charts, and combination charts

   d. Dashboard design: layout, formatting, labeling, and chart arrangement for a cohesive dashboard

2. Charts Included:

   a. Top Rated Products: shows products with the highest customer satisfaction

   b. Most Reviewed Products: highlights product popularity and sales volume

   c. Top Discounted Products: identifies products with the largest price reductions

   d. Discount vs Rating: combination chart comparing ratings and discount percentages

3. Key Insights:

   a. High-rating products do not always have the most reviews

   b. Many highly discounted products are concentrated in categories like chargers, cables, and earbuds

   c. Discounts do not strongly correlate with ratings

   d. Review count is a strong indicator of product popularity and sales volume

4. Tools Used:

   Microsoft Excel

   Pivot Tables

   Pivot Charts

   Data visualization and formatting techniques

Created as a beginner data analytics portfolio project to practice Excel dashboard creation and visualization
